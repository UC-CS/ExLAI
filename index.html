Welcome to ExLAI Project

Introduction: ExLAI aims to create pathways in Trustworthy Artificial Intelligence (TAI) for community college students.

Why ExLAI? The demand for AI professionals highlights the need for experiential learning in AI.

Key Stats: Impacting 180 scholars over three years through partnerships with four community colleges.

Program Pathways

Direct Career Pathway: 8-week training program preparing students for AI careers.

UC Immersion Pathway: Comprehensive AI training and co-op placements for continued education at UC.

Eligibility: Overview of eligibility criteria for each pathway.
